About Me

Lookman Ossam SANA

R&D Engineer in Oracle JD Edwards | Mckinsey Forward Program

Lookman is a young professional with a passion for digital transformation, strategy, leadership, and youth empowerment. Currently, Lookman is an R&D Engineer at Colas Digital Solutions, a member of the Bouygues Group. In his role, he analyses and coordinates meetings with clients worldwide to address technically their needs and develop innovative solutions.

An alumnus of the prestigious Ecole Nationale des Sciences Appliquées de Tanger (ENSAT) in Morocco, Lookman held several leadership roles during his studies. In 2022, he successfully coordinated two flagship events of the school: the JIG, the main event for international students, and the Cultural Club Day.

Lookman’s academic journey is marked by excellence. In 2018, he was among the top students in the national baccalaureate exam and the best student at his school. His outstanding performance earned him scholarships from both the government of Burkina Faso and the Kingdom of Morocco to pursue his studies in Morocco.

Beyond academics, Lookman has completed leadership & professional certifications from Harvard Kennedy School, Harvard Business School, Aspire Institute, Boston Consulting Group and McKinsey & Company. In recognition of his dedication and impact, he was selected as one of 150 young African leaders from more than 25 countries to participate in the Bridge Africa Summit in Benguerir in 2024.

On top of that, what matters more in the career of Lookman, is creating impact, and improving people’s lives.
